Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 18, 2026

1. Information We Collect

At GlacierGoals, we collect information to help you track your resolutions and provide an empathetic AI experience. This includes:

  • Account Information: Name and password used for login.
  • Usage Data: Interaction with the AI Penguin and habit completion data.
  • Diary Entries: Private thoughts stored in your "Secret Vault".

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the services offered by GlacierGoals. Specifically:

  • To personalize your AI interaction.
  • To track and visualize your progress over time.
  • To send reminders and notifications about your goals.

3. Data Security

We prioritize the security of your data. Your "Secret Vault" entries are protected and are not used for training AI models without explicit permission. We use industry-standard encryption to protect your account information.

4. Third-Party Services

We use reputable third-party services such as Groq for AI processing and Firebase for cloud notifications. These services may process data according to their own privacy policies.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through the app feedback section.
